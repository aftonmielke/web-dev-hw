DIY LIGHTBULB VASE

Welcome! This page will guide you along your way to creating your own adorably inventive recycled lightbulb vase!

Before Beginning, Gather Your Materials!

  • Safety Goggles
  • Twine or Other Strong/Decorative String
  • Needle Nose Pliers
  • Lightbulb
  • Small Flowers
First
The bottom of each lightbulb will have a round metal tab. Use your pliers to remove the metal tab.
Second
WIn order to get to the inside of the lightbulb, you will have to remove the black glass piece. To do this, DIY Projects recommends holding the lightbulb at the metal base so that you don’t break the bulb piece. Then, firmly tap the side of your metal pliers (you can use a hammer if you prefer) against the black glass. With a couple of hits, the black glass will break. Turn the lightbulb upside down so all the broken glass falls out.
